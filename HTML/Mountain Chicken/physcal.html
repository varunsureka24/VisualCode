Physical Characteristics of the Mountain Chicken

The mountain chicken is one of the largest frogs in the world. It can weigh up to 2.2 pounds and can reach up to 9 inches in length, although adults generally remain between 6-7 inches. Female mountain chickens are larger than males.

Mountain chickens are highly colorful. Their underside can vary from being chestnut-brown to plain to even spotted! Their sides can become slightly orange at times, and they have a dark, black line that runs from their snout to the corner of their mouth.
