Habitat of the Mountain Chicken

The mountain chicken used to be located on many of the smaller islands in the Caribbean Sea, but now it solely lives in Dominica and Montserrat. Even now the population of the mountain chicken is declining. Various volcanic activity on Montserrat has restricted their habitat solely to Centre Hill in northern Montserrat. Its habitat consists of moist, dense forests and scrubs, river valleys, and flooded forests. It is most commonly found near water and generally around lower altitudes.
